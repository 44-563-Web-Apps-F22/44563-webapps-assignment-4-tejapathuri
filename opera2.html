CHORUS

And thus, with sinning cloyed, Has died each Murgatroyd, And so shall fall, Both one and all, Each coming Murgatroyd!


ROB

Mistress Rose!


ROS

(Surprised.)

Master Robin!

ROB

I wished to say that—it is fine.


ROS

It is passing fine.


ROB

But we do want rain.


ROS

(Sighing.)

That is all

ROB

Good day, Master Rose!


ROS

Good day, Master Robin!


BOT

Now tell me pray, and tell me true, What in the world should the (young man\maiden) do?


ROS

He cannot eat and he cannot sleep—

(Hey, but his face is a sight for to see!)

Daily he goes for to wail—for to weep—

(Hey, but he's wretched as a youth can be!)

ROB

I wished to say that—it is fine.


ROS

It is passing fine.
